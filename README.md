# **CGPA Calculator**

**Description:**
CGPA Calculator is a simple and user-friendly Android app that helps students calculate their Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) quickly and accurately. Just enter your marks for each subject, and the app instantly computes your CGPA. Perfect for college and university students who want a fast and reliable way to track their academic performance.

---

## **Features**

* Clean and modern design
* Input marks for multiple subjects
* Instant CGPA calculation
* User-friendly interface


---

## **Installation**

1. Clone the repository or download the project zip.
2. Open in Android Studio.
3. Build and run the app on an Android device or emulator.

---

## **Usage**

1. Open the CGPA Calculator app.
2. Enter marks for Subject 1, Subject 2, and Subject 3.
3. Tap the **Calculate** button to get your CGPA.
